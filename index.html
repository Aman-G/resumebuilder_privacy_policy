Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 20, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Resume Maker, developed by Aman Grover ("we", "our", "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the app.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information

The following information is collected during sign-in via Firebase Authentication (Google Sign-In):

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Google account profile photo (used only within the app)

Resume Data

Resume content you enter — including work experience, education, skills, projects, certifications, and languages — is stored in Firebase Firestore (Google's cloud infrastructure) and is also cached locally on your device for offline access. This data is accessible only to you via your authenticated account.

Profile Photo

If you upload a profile photo from your device gallery or capture one using the camera, it is stored locally on your device only and is used solely for resume generation within the app. It is not uploaded to any server.

Purchase Data

When you make a one-time purchase via Google Play, your purchase status (plan type, access date, and Google Play purchase token) is stored in Firebase Firestore to manage your access to premium features. We do not store any card or banking details.

Usage Data

We collect anonymized usage data using:

  • Google Analytics for Firebase
  • Firebase Crashlytics

This may include device information, app activity, and crash logs.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and manage your account
  • To generate and customise your resumes
  • To sync your resume data across devices via Firebase Firestore
  • To manage your purchase and premium feature access
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs

4. AI-Powered Features

Resume Maker includes an optional AI summary generator. When you use this feature, relevant resume content (such as job title, skills, and work experience) is sent to Groq, Inc. (groq.com) to generate a professional summary. This data is processed by Groq in accordance with their Privacy Policy. Use of the AI feature is optional and no data is sent unless you explicitly tap "Generate with AI".

5. Payments

Premium templates are available via a one-time payment of ₹100 through Google Play Billing, which grants permanent lifetime access with no recurring or subscription charges. We do not collect or store your card or banking details — all payment transactions are handled securely by Google. Your purchase status is stored in Firebase Firestore solely to grant you access to premium features. Refunds are subject to Google Play's refund policy.

6. Permissions

  • Internet: Required for authentication, cloud sync, AI features, and analytics
  • Camera: Optional — used only to capture a profile photo within the app. Photos taken are stored locally on your device and are not uploaded to any server.
  • Storage / Media Images: Used to pick a profile photo from your gallery and to save exported PDF resumes to your device

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data is shared only with the following trusted third-party services that are necessary to operate the app:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Crashlytics, Analytics) — stores account info, resume data, and purchase status; processes crash and usage analytics
  • Google Play Billing — handles all payment transactions
  • Groq, Inc. — processes resume content only when you use the optional AI summary feature

8. Data Retention

Your name, email, resume data, and purchase status are retained in Firebase Firestore for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time (see Section 9).

9. Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data (name, email, resume content, and purchase status stored in Firebase Firestore) directly from within the app:

  1. Open the app and go to the Template Selection screen
  2. Tap the ⋮ menu (top-right corner)
  3. Select "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm in the dialog — your account and all data will be permanently deleted immediately

If you have uninstalled the app or are unable to access it, you can also request deletion by contacting us at:

📧 aman.grover42@gmail.com

Email requests will be processed within 30 days.

10. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including Firebase security rules that restrict access to your data to your authenticated account only. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

11. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
